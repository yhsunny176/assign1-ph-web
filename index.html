Bangladesh Achieves Milestone in Renewable Energy Production

Date: 11/1/2025

Time: 10.25 am

Bangladesh has reached a new milestone by generating 12% of its total electricity from renewable energy sources. With over 3,500 megawatts now produced through solar, wind, and hydroelectric power, the country is well on its way to achieving its ambitious target of 40% renewable energy by 2041. This achievement has been bolstered by significant investments in solar power infrastructure and partnerships with global organizations. Experts believe this progress underscores Bangladesh's commitment to sustainable development and combating climate change.

First-Ever Metro Rail Transforms Dhaka's Urban Transport

Date: 11/1/2025

Time: 10.25 am

Dhaka's much-anticipated metro rail has begun operations, marking a transformative moment for urban transportation in Bangladesh. The Mass Rapid Transit (MRT) Line 6, which connects Uttara to Motijheel, is expected to significantly reduce travel time and alleviate traffic congestion in the capital city.
